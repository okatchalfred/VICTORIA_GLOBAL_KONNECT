About Us

We are an international non-profit organization offering undergraduate and graduate students transformative opportunities to advance their careers. Through our medical programs in East Africa—Kenya, Uganda, Tanzania, Rwanda, Burundi, and South Sudan—participants gain hands-on experience while addressing critical healthcare needs in underserved communities. Join us to make a meaningful impact and grow in the field of healthcare.

MISSION

To bridge the gap in healthcare service delivery in underserved communities of Kenya and East Africa while providing an enriching practical learning experience for local and international students around the world in the medical field through our program.

VISION

To serve in merging the unable community and the first world countries to empower healthcare medical students by offering them transformative hands-on experience, fostering cross-cultural exchange to ensure a well sustainable environment.

Location

We are located in the heart of Nairobi, Kenya, a vibrant city in East Africa." Our office is situated in a central and easily accessible area, providing an ideal environment for international students who seek convenience, safety, and proximity to key amenities.
